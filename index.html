Create a Wallet

download metamask or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to metamask.io.

Get Some ETH or BNB

have ETH or BNB in your wallet to start $PPBTC Mining. If you don’t have any ETH or BNB, you can buy directly on metamask, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.
